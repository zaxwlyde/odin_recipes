Pepperoni Pizza!

Description

An all-time favorite for the family to enjoy. Get ready to sink your hands into some dough, smatter some sauce, melt delicious cheese, and delicately place slices of cut pepperoni!

NOTE: This recipe uses a high amount of fats, cheese, and gluten products to create a delicious pepperoni pizza. If you've suffered digestive issues with these ingredients in the past, we wouldn't recommend this recipe for you.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz pizza dough, this can be bought at the store, or made at home.
  • 1/2 cup of pizza sauce.
  • 18 to 20 slices of pepperoni.
  • 12 ounces of grated mozzarella cheese.
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground black pepper.
  • 1 teaspoon of fresh oregano.
  • Flour for rolling and shaping the dough.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 500Â°F.
  2. Roll the dough on a surface that's lightly floured. If rolling the dough is difficult, let it rest for roughly five minutes to reach room temperature.
  3. Using a lightly dusted pizza peel, shift the dough over.
  4. Add sauce lightly around the pizza, leaving roughly 1/4th inches of crust around the edges.
  5. Chop half of the pepperoni and sprinkle it over the sauce. Then, top the pizza with grated cheese and the rest of the pepperoni, before seasoning with black pepper.
  6. Cook your pizza for 6 minutes, then rotate it halfway for an even cooking. Proceed to cook the pizza for another 6 to 8 minutes, or until your crust is golden brown.
  7. The best part! Slide the pizza out, let it rest for a minute, and slice and dice your way to a delicious meal!
